EDIT 3/19/2025: Turns out, there's canon Cyber Style information-- both Obverse AND Reverse-- in The Valuable Book EX 5! I will pause in case anything debunks and/or contradicts with any of my headcanons, but do stay tuned in case there's a hole(s) to fill after the fact!

Upon reading The Machinations of Geartown that Helen wrote up for Chronos de Medici, I decided to try my hand at writing some headcanons for my characters' Duel Monsters. As much as I'd like to start with Marufuji Nii-san's Cyber Style Deck, @Marufuji-Shou is the one with that Deck... although as noted by @HelenaLevi, the Cyber Dragon part is strictly Onii-sama's, so I can start with those.

In General

The Cyber Style began a little before their dojo was founded. Even as they evolve, they hesitate to push others-- including their Duelists-- as far as their Reverse does. Yet, in battle, they give it their best, and their fastest. For some of the other concepts surrounding the Cyber Dragons, think a much less extreme Nazi Germany, i.e. aggressively pro-Machine/Cyborg instead of anit-Jew.

Cards are sorted by main card type (i.e. Monster/Spell/Trap), and then release date-- with real life releases overriding their air or publication date.
